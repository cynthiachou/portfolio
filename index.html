about me

Hi. My name is Yihan, an interaction designer living and working in New York City. I graduated from Parsons School of Design with a Master of Fine Arts degree in Design & Technology and am currently working as a fellow for Verizon. I am fond of all things whimsical, stunning design and great animation that makes me go weak in the knees.

Aside from drooling over food and design, you can find me either jogging at outdoors, out on another food adventure or hanging out with my dogs Meili and Batong.

I'd love to talk about working together.
